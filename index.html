A little about myself

Hi! My name is Emily, you can call me Emm or anything Emily-esque if you'd like. I am a somewhat of a nerd/geek of sorts. I enjoy sci-fi (this is including Star Trek), old school videogaming (N64!), drawing/designing, adventuring, dog training and most important of all: learning. A lot of my down time is spent absentmindedly doodling, paired up with watching National Geographic, the History Channel, or some other sort of documentary.

Over the past five years I've had the opportunity to explore the world, move to a different city, acquire two mutts: Kobayashi Maru and my rescue, Phoebe, graduate college and obtain a degree in Visual Publications, and grow and change completely as a person.

In the last three I've taken the initiative to teach myself how to code websites. Everything I know about coding I've learned on my own. I can currently hand code using HTML and CSS, and have dabbled in JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3. My motivation to learn to code originated from school during web design classes. They didn't teach us to how code, and were taught how to use Dreamweaver as a substitute. Unable to build the web pages as I wanted, using Dreamweaver templates, I went out on my own to find another way. With that, grew my curiosity in coding and brings me to where I am now.
